Detailed Description

mac_ax_role_info

struct mac_ax_role_info - role information : MAC ID. : Band selection, band0 or band1. : WMM selection, wmm0 ow wmm1. There are four sets about band and wmm, band0+wmm0, band0+wmm1, band1+wmm0,band1+wmm1.
